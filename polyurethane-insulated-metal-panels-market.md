<p><p>Polyurethane Insulated Metal Panels (PIMPs) are composite building materials consisting of a core of rigid polyurethane foam sandwiched between two sheets of metal. They are widely used in the construction industry for applications including walls, roofs, and cold storage facilities due to their excellent thermal insulation properties, energy efficiency, and lightweight nature. The PIMPs provide superior insulation and structural integrity, making them an attractive choice for both commercial and industrial buildings.</p><p>The Polyurethane Insulated Metal Panels Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.9% during the forecast period.
