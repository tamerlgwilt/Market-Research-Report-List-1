<p><p>The plant-based food colors market comprises various types, including carotenoids, anthocyanins, chlorophyll, paprika extract, and spirulina extract. Chlorophyll, extracted from green plants, gives a fresh green color. Paprika extract is derived from capsicum, delivering red shades, while spirulina extract offers a unique blue-green hue. These natural colorants are increasingly favored for their health benefits and clean-label appeal.</p></p>
