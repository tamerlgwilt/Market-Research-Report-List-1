<p><ul><li>Self-Destructive, Non-Self-Re-Closing</li><li>Re-Usable, Self-Re-Closing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rupture panels are safety devices designed to relieve pressure in industrial systems. The market includes self-destructive panels that permanently fail to prevent dangerous overpressure. Non-self-re-closing panels also fail once activated, requiring replacement. Re-usable panels can be reset after activation, making them cost-effective for repeated use. Self-re-closing panels allow for automatic resealing after an event, providing ongoing protection while minimizing downtime. Each type serves different safety and operational needs in various industries, such as oil, gas, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
