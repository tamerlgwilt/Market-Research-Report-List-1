<p><p>Software License Management (SLM) encompasses various enforcement types. Hardware-based enforcement relies on physical devices to control software access, ensuring compliance through specific hardware configurations. Software-based enforcement involves deploying software solutions that monitor and manage licenses, providing flexibility and integration with existing systems. Cloud-based enforcement utilizes cloud technology to manage licenses remotely, offering scalable solutions and real-time monitoring, enhancing accessibility and compliance for organizations. Each type addresses unique needs in managing software licenses effectively.</p></p>
