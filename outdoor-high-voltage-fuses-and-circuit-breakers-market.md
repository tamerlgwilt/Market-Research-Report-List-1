<p><p>The Outdoor High Voltage Fuses and Circuit Breakers market is categorized into two primary types: Outdoor Fuses and Outdoor Circuit Breakers. Outdoor fuses are designed to protect electrical systems from overloads and short circuits by melting a conductive element, thereby interrupting the current flow. In contrast, outdoor circuit breakers automatically disconnect electrical circuits during faults, providing a reusable solution. Both components are essential for maintaining the safety and reliability of outdoor electrical installations in various applications.</p></p>
