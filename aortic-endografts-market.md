<p><p>The aortic endografts market comprises several types designed for treating aortic aneurysms and dissections. Abdominal aortic endografts target aneurysms in the abdominal region, while thoracic aortic endografts address those in the thoracic segment. Fenestrated and branched endografts allow for natural blood flow to essential branches, accommodating complex anatomies. Flow diverting endografts focus on reducing blood flow in targeted areas, promoting aneurysm stabilization. These specialized devices enhance patient safety and outcomes in vascular interventions.</p></p>
