<p><p>The glass paint market is classified into two main types: water-based and solvent-based paints. Water-based glass paints use water as the primary solvent, offering easier cleanup and lower toxic emissions, making them eco-friendly and safe for indoor use. In contrast, solvent-based glass paints utilize organic solvents, providing superior adhesion and durability, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Each type caters to different customer preferences, project requirements, and environmental considerations, influencing their selection in various decorative and functional applications.</p></p>
