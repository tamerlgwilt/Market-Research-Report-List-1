<p><strong>The Marine Base Steering Gear Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ram Type</li><li>Rotary Vane Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Base Steering Gear market includes Ram Type and Rotary Vane Type systems. Ram Type steering gear utilizes hydraulic rams that provide direct linear motion, offering straightforward control and robust performance in larger vessels. In contrast, Rotary Vane Type steering gear operates using a rotating vane mechanism, allowing for compact designs and smoother operation, ideal for smaller ships or environments with space constraints. Both types are essential for effective navigation and stability in marine vessels, catering to different operational needs.</p></p>
