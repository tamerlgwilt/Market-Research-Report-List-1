<p><p>The Litz wire market comprises two primary types: silver-plated copper Litz wire and ordinary stranded Litz wire. Silver-plated copper Litz wire features a thin layer of silver coating over copper strands, enhancing conductivity and reducing skin effect at high frequencies, making it ideal for applications in radio frequency and high-performance devices. In contrast, ordinary stranded Litz wire consists of multiple insulated copper strands twisted together to minimize losses in alternating current applications, catering to general use in electrical and electronic devices.</p></p>
