<p><p>The injectable pain medication market primarily consists of two segments: Nonsteroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) and Central Analgesics. NSAIDs reduce inflammation and alleviate pain by inhibiting enzymes linked to the inflammatory process, commonly used in conditions like arthritis. Central analgesics, on the other hand, work on the central nervous system to relieve pain by altering pain perception. Both types are crucial for managing acute and chronic pain effectively, offering versatile options for healthcare providers in various clinical settings.</p></p>
