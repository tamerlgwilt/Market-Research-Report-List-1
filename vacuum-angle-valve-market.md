<p><p>The vacuum angle valve market consists of three main types: manual angle valves, pneumatic angle valves, and electromagnetic angle valves. Manual angle valves are operated by hand, allowing for precise flow control. Pneumatic angle valves utilize compressed air for remote operation, enhancing automation and efficiency in systems. Electromagnetic angle valves employ electromagnetic coils to control flow, providing rapid response times and high precision. Each type serves specific applications, catering to diverse industrial needs in vacuum systems.</p></p>
