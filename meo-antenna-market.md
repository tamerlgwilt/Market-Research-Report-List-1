<p><strong>The MEO Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEO antenna market comprises two main types: active and passive antennas. Active antennas incorporate amplifiers and signal processing capabilities, enhancing performance in reception and transmission, making them ideal for applications requiring higher gain and improved signal quality. Conversely, passive antennas lack electronic components and rely solely on their physical structure for signal reception and transmission. They are typically easier to deploy and maintain, but may offer lower performance compared to their active counterparts, catering to different market needs.</p></p>
