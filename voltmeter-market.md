<p><p>The voltmeter market is divided into two main types: DC voltage and AC voltage. DC voltmeters measure direct current voltage, commonly used in batteries, electronic circuits, and renewable energy systems. They provide stable and accurate readings for devices powered by direct current. In contrast, AC voltmeters measure alternating current voltage, essential for household appliances and industrial equipment. These meters help maintain optimal functionality in systems that vary in voltage and frequency, catering to diverse applications in electrical engineering and maintenance.</p></p>
