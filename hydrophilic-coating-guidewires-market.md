Straight guidewires are designed for direct navigation through vascular pathways, providing increased accessibility and reduced friction during procedures. In contrast, elbow guidewires feature a pre-formed curve that facilitates entry into complex anatomies, enhancing maneuverability in challenging cases. Both types utilize hydrophilic coatings to improve performance by minimizing friction and enhancing flexibility, ultimately contributing to better patient outcomes and procedural efficiency.</p></p>
