<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Industry Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry utilizes extruders for various applications, with two primary types: single screw and twin screw extruders. Single screw extruders consist of one screw and are typically used for simpler processes, offering cost-effectiveness and reliability. In contrast, twin screw extruders feature two intermeshing screws, allowing for better mixing, higher output, and versatility in processing various materials. The choice between the two types depends on the desired product characteristics, scale of production, and specific formulation requirements.</p></p>
