<p><p>The Small Business Network Switches market features two primary types: fixed port switches and modular switches. Fixed port switches offer a set number of Ethernet ports, ideal for small businesses with straightforward networking needs, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, modular switches allow businesses to customize their port configuration and capabilities, accommodating future growth and scalability. These switches are suitable for more complex networks, enabling the integration of additional ports or advanced features as requirements evolve.</p></p>
