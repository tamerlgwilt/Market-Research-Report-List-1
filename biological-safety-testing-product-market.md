<p><p>The biological safety testing product market encompasses various essential testing categories. Sterility testing products ensure that biological products are free from viable contaminants. Endotoxin testing products detect harmful endotoxins in pharmaceuticals. Cell line qualification and characterization testing ensure the integrity of cell lines used in research and production. Incidental reagent detection testing identifies unintended contaminants in reagents. Bioburden testing evaluates the presence of microbial life in non-sterile products. Residual host contamination detection testing assesses leftover materials from production processes, ensuring product safety.</p></p>
