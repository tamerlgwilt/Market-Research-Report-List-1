<p><p>The micro circuit breaker market is categorized into types based on the number of poles: 2-pole, 3-pole, and 4-pole breakers. 2-pole breakers are commonly used for single-phase applications, providing protection for both live and neutral wires. 3-pole breakers are ideal for three-phase systems, ensuring balanced load distribution and protection. 4-pole breakers are utilized in three-phase systems with a neutral, accommodating complex circuits or systems requiring additional safety measures. Each type serves specific electrical needs and applications.</p></p>
