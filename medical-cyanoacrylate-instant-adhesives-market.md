<p><p>The Medical Cyanoacrylate Instant Adhesives market is categorized by viscosity levels, which significantly influence their applications in medical settings. Adhesives with viscosity under 100 cps are typically low-viscosity formulations, suitable for fine applications such as skin closure and internal tissue adhesion. Adhesives with viscosities between 100-1000 cps provide a balance of adhesion and control, making them ideal for surgical procedures. Those with viscosities above 1000 cps offer greater strength and gap-filling properties, suitable for more demanding applications and wound management.</p></p>
