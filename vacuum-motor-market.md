<p><p>The vacuum motor market comprises primarily two types: vacuum stepping motors and vacuum servo motors. Vacuum stepping motors are designed for precise control and positioning in applications requiring incremental movement, commonly used in robotics and automated systems. In contrast, vacuum servo motors provide high-speed operation and enhanced torque control, making them ideal for dynamic applications such as packaging and assembly lines. Both types are vital in industries where efficiency and precision in a vacuum environment are essential.</p></p>
