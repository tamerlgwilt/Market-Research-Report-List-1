<p><p>The High Speed Textile Digital Printing Machine market includes two primary types: rotary screen and flat screen printing. Rotary screen printing utilizes cylindrical screens for continuous and high-volume production, ideal for intricate designs and patterns. It offers faster printing speeds compared to traditional methods. Flat screen printing, on the other hand, involves flat screens and is more flexible, allowing for detailed printing on various fabric types. Both types cater to different printing needs, enhancing productivity and design capabilities in the textile industry.</p></p>
