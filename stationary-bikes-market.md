<p><p>The stationary bike market consists of three primary types: indoor cycling bikes, upright bikes, and recumbent bikes. Indoor cycling bikes are designed for high-intensity workouts, offering a race-like experience with adjustable resistance. Upright bikes resemble traditional bicycles and provide a more standing position, focusing on cardio fitness. Recumbent bikes feature a reclined seating position, providing enhanced comfort and lower back support, making them ideal for users with mobility issues or those seeking a gentler workout. Each type caters to different fitness needs and preferences.</p></p>
