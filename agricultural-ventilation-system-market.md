<p><p>The agricultural ventilation system market encompasses various types, including roof, ridge, and floor-mounted systems. Roof-mounted systems utilize natural airflow and fans to regulate temperature and humidity in barns and greenhouses, enhancing livestock and crop health. Ridge ventilation systems are placed along the apex of structures, promoting effective air circulation and reducing moisture buildup. Floor-mounted systems, often equipped with fans, facilitate even air distribution across the entire area, ensuring optimal growing conditions. Each type caters to specific agricultural needs for improved productivity.</p></p>
