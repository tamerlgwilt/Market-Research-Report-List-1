<p><p>The car covers market can be divided into two main types: custom car covers and universal car covers. Custom car covers are designed to fit specific vehicle models, offering tailored protection and a snug fit. They often provide superior durability and weather resistance. In contrast, universal car covers are designed to fit a range of vehicle sizes and types, making them more versatile but potentially less precise in fit. Both types serve to protect vehicles from environmental elements and potential damage.</p></p>
