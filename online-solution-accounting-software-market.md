<p><strong>The Online Solution Accounting Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C/S(Client/Server)</li><li>B/S(Browser/Server)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Online Solution Accounting Software market is categorized into two primary types: Client/Server (C/S) and Browser/Server (B/S). C/S architecture involves a centralized server hosting the software while clients access it through dedicated applications, offering robust performance and security. In contrast, B/S architecture operates through web browsers, enabling easier access and flexibility from any device with internet connectivity. While C/S is often favored for complex functionalities, B/S is popular for its user-friendly interface and cost-effectiveness in deployment.</p></p>
